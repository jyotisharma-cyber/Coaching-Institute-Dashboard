##  Project Overview

The Coaching Institute Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to help educational institutions monitor student performance, attendance, enrollment status, academic progress, and learning engagement.

The dashboard provides a centralized view of key student metrics, enabling administrators, faculty members, and management teams to make informed decisions that improve academic outcomes and student retention.

---

##  Business Objectives

- Monitor overall student performance.
- Analyze attendance and GPA trends.
- Track enrollment status across students.
- Identify at-risk students for early intervention.
- Measure LMS (Learning Management System) engagement.
- Compare academic performance across majors.

##  Dashboard Features

###  Student Overview

Provides a high-level summary of institutional performance:

- Total Students
- Average GPA
- Average Attendance
- Average Grade
- At-Risk Students
- LMS Activity

---

###  Student Distribution by Major

Visualizes student enrollment across majors:

- Arts
- Computer Science
- Engineering
- Business

This helps identify the most popular academic programs.

---

###  Enrollment Status Distribution

Analyzes student enrollment categories:

- Active Students
- Graduated Students
- Students on Leave

Useful for monitoring retention and completion rates.

---

###  Average GPA by Major

Compares academic performance across different majors.

This helps evaluate which academic programs are performing well.

---

###  Attendance vs GPA Analysis

Examines the relationship between attendance and academic achievement.

Key Questions Answered:

- Does higher attendance lead to better GPA?
- Which majors demonstrate strong academic engagement?
- How does attendance influence student success?

---

###  Risk Analytics

Identifies students requiring academic support based on:

- GPA
- Attendance
- LMS Activity
- Academic Performance

Risk Categories:

- High Risk
- Medium Risk
- Low Risk

---

###  LMS Analytics

Measures student engagement with online learning platforms.

Used to monitor:

- Learning participation

- Student activity levels

##  Key Insights

### Student Performance

- The institute serves **2,000 students**.
- Average GPA is **3.01**, indicating stable academic performance.
- Average grade score is approximately **80%**.

### Attendance Analysis

- Average attendance rate is **79%**.
- Students with consistent attendance generally achieve stronger academic results.

### Enrollment Analysis

- Most students are actively enrolled.
- Graduation and leave status provide insights into student progression and retention.

### Major Analysis

- Student enrollment is fairly distributed among Arts, Engineering, Business, and Computer Science programs.
- Academic performance remains consistent across majors.

### Risk Analysis

- More than **800 students** are classified as at-risk.
- Early intervention programs can improve academic success and retention.

### LMS Engagement

- LMS activity helps track student participation in online learning environments.
- Engagement metrics support data-driven academic planning.
